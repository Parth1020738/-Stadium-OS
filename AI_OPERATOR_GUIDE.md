# AI Stadium Copilot - Operator User Guide

Welcome to your stadium operations command assistant. The Copilot enables natural-language control of the stadium, allowing you to query metrics and execute actions without navigating multiple screens.

## Quick Start Actions

1. **Gate Flow Ingress**:
   - Query: *"Why is Gate D crowded?"*
   - Operational Action: Click **[Execute]** under recommendations to open secondary gates.
2. **Shift Allocation Checks**:
   - Query: *"Which volunteers are overloaded?"* or *"Show volunteer shortages."*
   - Operational Action: Click **[Assign]** to dispatch volunteer reinforcements.
3. **Transit Shuttle Delays**:
   - Query: *"Predict transit delays."* or *"Recommend transport improvements."*
   - Operational Action: Click **[Approve]** to post delay notices to guest digital boards.

## Interactive Command feedback
When executing operational commands directly from the Copilot page, a status feedback banner is displayed at the top of the interface showing audit logs (e.g. `Command "Open Gate D" [Action: EXECUTE] successfully sent to system`).
